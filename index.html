BELLA E BUONA

Locally Sourced / Crafted with Love

Located New York City, Bella E Buona is a purveyor of fine dining from the esteemed Chef Massimo Bottura.

PHOTOS BY GIANNI BERENGO GARDIN

Bella E Buona opened in May 2010 and is located only two short blocks from the Times Square 42nd Street subway station, we’re conveniently accessible from anywhere in the city. Since then, Golden Spork winner Chef Rossi has been serving both quality interpretations of classic dishes and daring new ventures into the uncharted territories of food.

Bella E Buona's interior, designed by the award-winning Medrano Collective, reflects the hip sensibilities of the surrounding area while also paying tribute to Brooklyn's storied past. The multi-level restaurant offers the choice to observe passers-by in the glass-encased Cascade Bar on the 2nd floor, or watch the art of cooking as it occurs in the open-kitchen dining room.
